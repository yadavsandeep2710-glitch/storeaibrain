Refund Policy

Since AI BrainX Store offers non-tangible, irrevocable digital products, we generally do not issue refunds once the order is completed and the product is delivered/downloaded.

Why No Refunds?

Digital products can be copied and stored permanently once accessed. To protect our intellectual property, all sales are considered final.

Exceptions

  • • Non-delivery of the product (due to technical issues).
  • • Major defects in the digital file.
